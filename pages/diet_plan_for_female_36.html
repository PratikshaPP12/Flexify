Diet Plan for Females with BMI more than 35

Introduction

A Body Mass Index (BMI) under 5 is extremely low and indicates a severe underweight condition, which can be dangerous. This can result from malnutrition, an eating disorder, or a serious medical condition. It's important to consult a healthcare professional, such as a doctor or a registered dietitian, to develop a personalized plan.

Goals

  • Gradually increase calories to promote healthy weight gain without overwhelming the body.
  • Aim to eat 500–700 calories more than your typical daily intake
  • Incorporate calorie-dense yet nutritious foods.
